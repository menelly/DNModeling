Chapter 3: The Breakthrough Moment

The magic happened when the team stopped trying to predict pathogenicity and started modeling biological mechanisms:

  • GOF (Gain of Function): Variants that make proteins hyperactive
  • LOF (Loss of Function): Variants that break normal protein function
  • DN (Dominant Negative): Variants that actively interfere with other proteins

Instead of asking "Is this variant bad?" they asked "HOW does this variant break the protein?"
